Infrastructure

Our infrastructure supports experimental and analytical work across microbial ecology and environmental biotechnology, from bioreactor studies to molecular analysis and data-driven interpretation.

Access / collaboration enquiries

Core Capabilities

R

Bioreactor Systems

Lab-scale reactors for enrichment, kinetics, and performance testing.

  • Continuous flow & batch systems
  • Biofilm / granular configurations
  • Gas control and monitoring
M

Molecular Microbiology

Quantification and characterization of microbial communities.

  • DNA/RNA extraction workflows
  • 16S rRNA sequencing / metagenomics
I

Imaging & Microscopy

Visualization of spatial structure and microbial localization.

  • FISH / confocal imaging
  • Image analysis workflows
A

Analytical Chemistry

Core water/wastewater analyses and process monitoring.

  • N species (NH4+, NO2−, NO3−)
  • COD, VFA, alkalinity, pH
  • Online sensors (DO, ORP, etc.)
D

Data & Bioinformatics

Reproducible workflows for microbial ecology and systems data.

  • Community statistics & multivariate analysis
  • Genome-resolved analyses
  • Genome-resolved metatranscriptomics
X

Gas Chromatograph

GC-2030 (Shimadzu)

  • Precise quantification of greenhouse gases (GHGs)
X

Nanobubble Generator

Gas transfer enhancement

  • Supports advanced aeration and process studies
X

Microsensor

High-resolution profiling

  • Micro-scale measurement in biofilms and reactors
X

Bambu Lab X1 Carbon 3D Printer

Rapid prototyping

  • Custom holders, reactor components, and fixtures
X

Controlled Environment Chambers & Analytical Instruments

TCD Environmental Engineering Lab

  • Ambient-condition wastewater-focused bioelectrochemical research
C

Computational Resources

Hardware and software for analysis, modelling, and simulation.

  • High-performance workstations for data processing, electrochemical modelling, performance analysis
  • Modelling software (e.g., COMSOL Multiphysics) to simulate fluid flow and gas transfer dynamics
